The Complete Guide to Renting an Apartment in Surabaya: Budget, Location, and What to Watch Out For

May 20, 2026 10 min read By RESIDIA Editorial Team

Renting an apartment in Surabaya can be exciting, especially for students, young professionals, and families who want easier access to the city’s education, business, and lifestyle areas. However, choosing the right apartment is not only about finding a beautiful room or the cheapest rent.

A good rental decision should consider budget, location, facilities, contract terms, and hidden costs. This guide helps you understand what to prepare before choosing an apartment in Surabaya.

1 Set a Realistic Monthly Budget

Before looking at listings, decide how much you can comfortably spend each month. Rent is only one part of the total cost. You may also need to pay for electricity, water, internet, parking, maintenance fees, and a security deposit.

Budget checklist:

  • Monthly rent
  • Electricity and water bills
  • Internet subscription
  • Parking or service charge
  • Security deposit and admin fee

Pro Tip:

Do not judge an apartment only by its listed rent. Always calculate the full monthly cost so you do not get surprised after moving in.

2 Choose the Right Surabaya Area

Surabaya has different areas with different living styles. East Surabaya is popular for students and young professionals because it is close to universities and growing commercial areas. Central Surabaya offers easier access to offices, malls, and public facilities. West Surabaya is known for modern residences, lifestyle centers, and premium apartments.

Good for students:

  • Close to campus
  • Affordable food options
  • Easy online transportation access
  • Nearby minimarkets and laundry

Good for professionals:

  • Close to office districts
  • Easy commute routes
  • Parking availability
  • Secure building access

3 Compare Facilities and Unit Condition

Facilities can greatly affect your daily comfort. Some units are fully furnished, while others only provide basic furniture. Check whether the unit includes a bed, wardrobe, air conditioner, kitchen set, washing machine, Wi-Fi access, balcony, and parking access.

Things to verify:

  • Are the photos updated?
  • Is the unit fully furnished or semi furnished?
  • Does the AC work properly?
  • Is the bathroom clean and functional?
  • Is there enough storage space?

4 Understand the Lease and Payment Rules

Before confirming a booking, make sure you understand the rental period, payment deadline, deposit policy, cancellation rule, and maintenance responsibility. A clear agreement protects both the renter and the property owner.

Watch Out:

Be careful if the lease terms are unclear, if the deposit policy is not written, or if the owner refuses to provide a proper payment receipt.

5 Avoid Hidden Fees

Some apartments may look affordable at first, but the final monthly cost can increase because of extra charges. Always ask for a full price breakdown before paying the deposit or confirming the booking.

Common hidden fees:

  • Service charge
  • Cleaning fee
  • Parking fee
  • Internet fee
  • Move-in administration fee

6 Use a Verified Rental Platform

Searching through scattered social media posts can be risky because information may be incomplete or outdated. A verified rental platform helps renters compare listings, view complete details, save favorite apartments, book units, and make payments in one organized flow.

Why RESIDIA helps:

RESIDIA focuses on verified listings, transparent pricing, easy booking, and trusted property partners so renters can make decisions with more confidence.

Final Thoughts: Rent Smarter in Surabaya

Finding the right apartment in Surabaya becomes easier when you know what to compare. Start from your budget, choose a strategic location, check the facilities, understand the lease, and avoid hidden costs.

Quick Checklist:

  • Set your full monthly budget
  • Compare Surabaya areas based on your lifestyle
  • Check facilities and real unit condition
  • Read lease and deposit terms carefully
  • Ask for a full price breakdown
  • Use verified listings whenever possible
